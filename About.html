About Us

Founded by Brian Cronin in 2008, Goldsmith Studios now includes Donnie Bott, Nicholas Layman, Sammy Shaheen.

We offer 5 great sounding hardwood floor rooms and 2 ISO booths (1 super live & 1 very dead) to capture, in a very comfortable environment, with the ability to record up to 16 channels at a time into Pro Tools.
